Welcome to my academic website! I am Haofei Xu, a Ph.D. student in Computational and Data Science at Washington University in St. Louis. My research lies at the intersection of computer vision and machine learning, where I develop innovative algorithms and models to solve complex visual understanding problems.

I bring a unique interdisciplinary perspective to my work, combining my background in social psychology with computer science and statistics. This diverse foundation allows me to approach technical challenges with both analytical rigor and an understanding of human behavior and cognition.

Currently, I am focused on advancing the field of computer vision through deep learning methodologies, with particular interest in developing robust and interpretable AI systems. My work spans both theoretical foundations and practical applications of machine learning in visual recognition tasks.

Research Interests
======
My research focuses on:

- Deep Learning and Neural Networks
- Machine Learning Theory and Applications
- Computational Data Science
- AI Interpretability and Robustness
